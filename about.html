- About Us -

UrbanPromise Trenton is a non-profit located in Trenton NJ that provides free AfterSchool and Summer Camp Programs with a strong focus on academics. UrbanPromise Trenton also run the StreetLeader Program which employs teens as mentors to the younger students while receiving academic support. As these students move through high school, they can enroll in the Youth College Readiness Program which helps them prepare for college. After the students graduate and are in college, UrbanPromise maintains contact by providing tutors throughout their college career.

Working with Princeton University students, this tutoring portal has been developed to connect students and tutors directly to make the entire process efficient and seamless. Using this portal, students will be matched based on their study needs with a tutor that specializes in their subject.
